MANGA NOVEL DOSSIER SPECIAL ARTS

Zen (The Transcended)

Origin: Earth (Survivor of the Gamma Burst).

Species: Sentinel

Power Level: Semi-Prime (Rapidly evolving to Alpha).

Abilities: Superhuman and insane straight with similar powers has primes

Heavy Grip/Fist: Increases his density to deliver mountain-shattering blows.

Gamma Durability: High resistance to radiation and physical trauma.

Personality: Humble, protective, and fueled by the memory of his parents. He is the bridge between Human empathy and Prime power.

Bruce (The General)

Origin: Unknown (High-ranking Solar Sentinel).

Role: Commander of the Sentinel Training Camp.

Details: Possesses immense physical strength (can lift boulders with one hand). He is the strategist who sees the bigger picture of the war against the Primes.

Miguel (Alpha Prime)

Rank: Alpha (Top 3 strongest Alphas).

Personality: Arrogant, brutal, and follows a strict code of orders.

Details: He nearly killed Daniel and sees Zen as a "spark" with potential. He doesn't kill for fun, but for the mission of the Primes.

Daniel (Star Vessel)

Origin: Omnis-7.

Role: Senior Tactical Member / Tech Genius.

Abilities: * Nano-Tech Suit: A liquid-metal armor that responds to neural commands.

Drill Fist: High-speed rotational kinetic strikes.

Tactical AI: Analyzes enemy movements and structural weaknesses.

Personality: Cocky and confident, but deeply loyal. He recently learned humility after his brutal loss to Miguel.
